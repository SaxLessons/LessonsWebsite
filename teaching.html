Teaching

Pedagogy

I believe I take a more relaxed approach than most other people in my approach to teaching. While the relationship between myself and a student is, formally, a student-teacher relationship, we both have ways to go in learning and mastering the saxophone. Just as my saxophone teachers have been able to help me by revealing the little mistakes I make that can make the biggest impacts in my performance, I want to give the mistakes that I can notice being made by a student while teaching them that can have the greatest impact of their performance. I currently only teach students at the upper middle school and high school levels, though arrangements can be made for students outside of that range. I also only currently teach in-person lessons, as I do not believe myself capable of teaching in a virtual setting at this moment.

Lesson Rates

The lesson rate will be $10 per 30 minutes of lessons, or $20 per hour. I am currently not offering lessons that are outside of those timeframes. Payment will be accepted on the day of the lessons if it is physical cash or within 1 day of the lesson day and time if paid digitally. Digital payment methods can be discussed between me and the student/parent(s) as required.

Lesson Policy

Late Policy

If a student is late, I expect the student to still show up and be at their lesson for however long they can be if the student can arrive within their normal lesson time. While exceptions can be made on a case-by-case basis, I generally expect payment in full in the instance of a late lesson. If a lesson cannot be held and adequate time (roughly 24 hours before the day and time of the lesson) is given, the lesson will generally be cancelled and no payments will be required for that week. If the student/parent(s) are paying in cash, I expect the total payment for the previous lesson and current lesson at the start of the next lesson. The timeframe for online payment is not affected by the student being late for or missing a lesson.

Makeup Policy

In order for the student to make up a lesson, the student or their guardian(s) must contact me (see on "Contacts" page) within 24 hours before the normally scheduled lesson time. If I, for whatever reason, need to reschedule a lesson, I will be in contact as soon as possible to reschedule; I will likely contact via email or by cell phone if this is the case. The same payment and lesson procedures for normal lessons will be followed in the case of a make up lesson, with the payment shifted to match the new lesson day and time.

Lesson Materials

The lessons material will vary on the needs of the student and the goals set for them, but do not expect a requirement of buying materials for lessons unless the goals of the student would require it. If external materials are required, I will first try what material(s) are needed and the best place to order it. If the cost of extra materials is something the student/gaurdians cannot hold themselves, arrangements can be made to offset the costs on the student(s)/guardian(s)
